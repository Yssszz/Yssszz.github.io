Is Trading a Scam?

Author N.John

The question of whether trading is a scam has been debated for decades, with millions of people losing their savings every year to bad trades, fraudulent brokers, and get-rich-quick schemes.

Financial experts warn that the majority of retail traders lose money in the long run, with studies suggesting that up to 80% of day traders end up with losses after one year of trading.

Social media has made the problem worse, with countless influencers promoting trading courses and signals that promise guaranteed profits in exchange for hefty subscription fees.

However, legitimate trading does exist. Professional traders and institutional investors make consistent profits through years of experience, discipline, and risk management strategies that most beginners lack.

The bottom line — trading itself is not a scam, but the industry is filled with scammers. Always do your research before investing your hard-earned money.

Is Epstein Still Alive?

Author D.Trump

The death of Jeffrey Epstein in August 2019 remains one of the most controversial and widely debated events in recent American history, with millions of people refusing to believe the official story.

Epstein was found dead in his prison cell at the Metropolitan Correctional Center in New York, with authorities ruling his death as suicide by hanging. However, many experts and observers have questioned this conclusion.

Forensic pathologist Dr. Michael Baden, who was hired by Epstein's family to observe the autopsy, concluded that the injuries were more consistent with homicide than suicide.

Adding fuel to the conspiracy theories, surveillance cameras outside Epstein's cell were reportedly malfunctioning on the night of his death, and two prison guards who were supposed to check on him had fallen asleep.

To this day, many people believe that Epstein was murdered to protect powerful individuals connected to his alleged trafficking network, while others maintain that he is still alive and living under a secret identity somewhere in the world.
